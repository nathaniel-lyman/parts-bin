Page-level title block with eyebrow, description, and actions slot.

**When to use:** Top of a full page or route to establish title and primary actions.

**Key props:** `eyebrow`, `title`, `description`, `actions`

**Example:**
```tsx
<PageHeader title="Projects" description="Track active work" actions={<Button>New</Button>} />
```
