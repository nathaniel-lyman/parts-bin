Centered empty/zero-data message with glyph, title, and optional action.

**When to use:** A list, table, or panel has no data to show.

**Key props:** `title`, `description`, `action`, `glyph`

**Example:**
```tsx
<EmptyState title="No projects" description="Create your first project." action={<Button>New</Button>} />
```
