Chronological list of activity events with tone, actor, and timestamp.

**When to use:** A stream of recent events or actions on a record.

**Key props:** `items`, `title`, `emptyState`, `className`

**vs Timeline:** Use Timeline for a connected, milestone-style vertical history.

**Example:**
```tsx
<ActivityFeed title="Activity" items={events} />
```
